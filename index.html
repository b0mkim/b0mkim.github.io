About

I am an M.S. student at the Cybersecurity Convergence Laboratory (CCLAB), Incheon National University. My research interests include serverless security, least-privilege IAM enforcement, and cloud-native security (e.g., Kubernetes network/system policy).

Research Interests

  • Serverless security: least-privilege enforcement, permission-bypass detection, runtime verification
  • Cloud-native security: Kubernetes networking, policy generation, eBPF/XDP-based enforcement
  • Practical security automation: analysis-to-policy pipelines, evaluation on real deployments
